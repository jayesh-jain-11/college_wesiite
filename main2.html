WEEKEND EXECUTIVE MANAGEMENT PROGRAM


EXECUTIVE MANAGEMENT PROGRAM @ BMSCCM

BMS Centre for Executive Education & Distance Learning (BMSCEEDL) in strategic alliance with All India Management Association (AIMA), an apex body of Management profession in India is offering their 24 months part-time Executive Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) course in Bangalore. The AIMA-CEM, PGDM programme is AICTE and MHRD approved. This is a unique blend of quality and flexibility from AIMA and academic excellence from BMS. The program is designed for working executives who has aspiration to study while working. The program is delivered in face-to-face mode and online on weekends. Students who take this course are young, energetic and has enthusiasm to achieve great heights in life. During the two years of the program students get to study syllabus as prescribed by AIMA and they can also choose to study career enhancement add on courses like Executive Communication, International Economic scenario, Business Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence.


CA/CS/CMA(ICWA) Coaching Classes


CA/CS/CMA(ICWA) Coaching Classes @ BMSCCM

BMS College of Commerce & Managements in association with Ace Trisha Education Pvt. Ltd (K2 Learning) offers coaching to CA and CS aspirants. The students opting for degree courses can additionally opt for these professional so that they can equip themselves with professional career as soon as they finish the University Program. The course focuses on quality coaching with the help of faculty members who are professionals with an extensive flair for teaching. Most of the teaching faculties are practicing professionals such as Chartered Accountant and Company Secretary. The CA/CS classes provides personalised attention to all the students who join these coaching classes. The pass percentage at the academy has been an inspiration to the students to join the academy for next level coaching. The classes for CA and CS are offered for Foundation, Intermediate & Final Levels.
